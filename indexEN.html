About

Villa Marija is a modern tourist facility, located in the city center in a quiet area Bregovi. Villa Marija has a capacity of 51 beds, consisting of 15 studio apartments. Apartments are spacious, well-lit, with excellent sound and thermal insulation. With a modern interior, the entire facility is equipped with optical internet. The Old Town of Trebinje and the recognizable Platani garden are less than 100 meters from the property. Local bus station and bank are located in range of 100 m away from facility. Apartments Vila Marija is 21 km away from Dubrovnik and 40 km away from Herceg Novi, while Marina and Dubrovnik Airport are 30 km away.
